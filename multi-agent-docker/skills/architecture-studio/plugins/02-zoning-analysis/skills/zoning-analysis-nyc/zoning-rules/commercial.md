## Bulk Controls

### Height Factor (standard C districts without suffix)
Same system as equivalent R districts:
- No absolute height limit
- Sky exposure plane controls
- Open space ratio for residential portions

### Contextual (C districts with A/B/D/X suffix)
Same system as equivalent R contextual district — see `contextual-districts.md`.

### C5 Districts (Central Commercial)
Special rules for the Manhattan core:
- No height limit (C5-2 and above)
- Mandatory setback above base height
- Tower coverage rules apply
- Bonus FAR available (transit, plaza, etc.)

## Commercial Overlays (C1 and C2 in Residential Districts)

Commercial overlays allow local retail and service uses in residential neighborhoods without changing the underlying residential zoning.

### How Overlays Work

1. Overlays are mapped as strips along commercial streets within R districts
2. Commercial uses are limited to:
   - **Ground floor** (and second floor in some overlays)
   - **150 ft depth** from the street line (C1/C2 overlays only)
3. The underlying residential district rules continue to control building bulk, height, and density
4. The overlay adds commercial FAR **on top of** residential FAR (up to a combined maximum)

### Permitted Uses in Overlays

**C1 overlays (Use Groups 5–9):**
- Retail stores up to 10,000 SF
- Eating/drinking establishments
- Personal service (barber, salon, dry cleaner)
- Professional offices (doctor, dentist, small office)
- Banks, loan offices

**C2 overlays (Use Groups 5–14):**
- Everything in C1 plus:
- Funeral homes
- Repair services
- Larger offices
- Photo studios, gyms

### Overlay Depth
Commercial use is typically limited to 150 ft from the street line. Beyond 150 ft, only residential and community facility uses are permitted even on the overlay-zoned portion.

## Rear Yard and Yard Requirements

Commercial districts generally follow the same rear yard rules as their equivalent residential district:
- 30 ft rear yard for residential portions
- 20 ft rear yard for commercial-only buildings (C1–C6)
- No rear yard required for M-equivalent C8 districts

## Loading Berths

Required for buildings with large amounts of commercial floor area:
- 1 berth for 15,000–40,000 SF of commercial
- Additional berths for larger buildings
- Specific dimensions required (33 ft × 12 ft × 14 ft minimum)

See `parking.md` for current parking/loading requirements.
