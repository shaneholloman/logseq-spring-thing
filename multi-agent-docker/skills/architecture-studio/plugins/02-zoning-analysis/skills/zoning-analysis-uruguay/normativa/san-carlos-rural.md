# San Carlos — Suelo Rural

## Source Regulations
- **Resolución 3103/2014** — rural building parameters by zone
- **Decreto 3866/2010** — rural soil categorization (SRN = Rural Natural, SRP = Rural Productivo)
- **Decreto 3867/2010** — departmental guidelines, min 45,000 m² per dwelling
- **Decreto 3718/1997 (TONE)** — general provisions (Sección I) apply; Art. 2 scope; Art. 170 water easement

## TONE Applicability to Rural Land (Art. 2)
The TONE applies to rural areas:
- South of Ruta Nacional No. 9
- Within 50m of national routes, departmental roads, or neighborhood roads
- Any other area at municipal discretion
- All rural construction not specifically for owner housing, staff residences, or storage

## Rural Zone Classifications (Resolución 3103/2014)

All zones share: **FOS 5% · FOT 8% · 90% natural unpaved**

| Zone | Description | Setbacks | Notes |
|------|-------------|----------|-------|
| Chacras de Recreo | Recreation farms (5+ ha) | — | General rural |
| SR | Coastal Zone Rural | 20 m all sides | Near coast |
| ZII | Maritime Chacras Rural | 25 m all sides | Most of San Carlos rural area |
| ZIII | Northern Chacras Rural | 30 m all sides | Further inland |
| ZIV | Priority Environmental | 40 m all sides | Restricted; only via integrated development programs |

## Building Parameters (all rural zones)

### Occupation Factors
| Factor | Value |
|--------|-------|
| FOS | 5% |
| FOT | 8% |
| Natural unpaved surface | 90% minimum |

### Height
- Maximum: **7 m** (PB + 1 piso)

### Dwelling Density
- Minimum **50,000 m² per dwelling unit** (Decreto 3867: 45,000 m²; Resolución 3103: 50,000 m²)
- Minimum **20 m separation** between units (if multiple dwellings on large lots)

### Setbacks (by zone)
- SR: 20 m from all property boundaries
- ZII: 25 m from all property boundaries
- ZIII: 30 m from all property boundaries
- ZIV: 40 m from all property boundaries

### Water Course Easement (Art. 170 TONE)
- **150 m non-building easement** from public waterways, measured from waterline or upper bank

## Permitted Uses
- Agricultural production
- One residential dwelling per 50,000 m² (isolated unit)
- Auxiliary rural buildings (sheds, storage, barns) — not counted as dwellings
- "High naturalness" preservation required
- **No urbanization** — rural land cannot be subdivided for urban purposes without soil category transformation

## Soil Category Transformation (Decreto 3866/2010)
- Rural → Suburban possible only when luxury dwellings (categories D/E per TONE Art. 41) already exist
- Requires Executive approval
- 25m buffer zone from public domain boundary required after transformation
- 15m minimum service road width
- SRN (Rural Natural) land cannot be classified as "potentially transformable"

## Key Restrictions
- Rural soil cannot contain urbanizations
- No residential subdivisions without prior land transformation
- Building authorization (permiso de construcción) mandatory
- 90% of lot must remain natural/unpaved
- Only isolated residential units permitted (no blocks, no paired units)
