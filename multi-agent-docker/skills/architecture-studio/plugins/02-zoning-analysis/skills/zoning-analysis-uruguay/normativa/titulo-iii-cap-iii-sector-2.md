## Art. D.268 — Límites del sector y divisiones

Sector boundaries: Laguna José Ignacio / Arroyo José Ignacio (W/SW), 500m north of Ruta 9 (N/NW), Arroyo Garzón / Laguna Garzón (E/NE, border with Rocha), Atlantic Ocean (S/SE).

### Zone structure

- **Zona 2.1 — Costera Sur**
  - 2.1.1 Pueblo José Ignacio
    - 2.1.1.1 Amanzanado fundacional
    - 2.1.1.2 Manzanas de fragilidad ecosistémica (mznas. 34, 28, 22)
  - 2.1.2 Barrios Jardín
    - 2.1.2.1 Áreas balnearias urbanizadas
    - 2.1.2.2 La Juanita
  - 2.1.3 Resto de la Zona Costera Sur
    - 2.1.3.1 Suelo Suburbano
    - 2.1.3.2 Suelo Rural
- **Zona 2.2 — Chacras Marítimas** (low/med/high density)
- **Zona 2.3 — Chacras del Norte** (low/med density)
- **Zona 2.4 — Prioridad Ambiental**
- **Zona 2.5 — Protección de Cuerpos de Agua**
- **Zona 2.6 — Banda de Resguardo**

---

## Art. D.268.2 — Condiciones generales

- **Block buildings prohibited** sector-wide unless the specific zone authorizes them.
- **Wastewater:** Lots < 4,000 m² must connect to public sewage network (authorization from Intendencia). Larger lots require custom approved disposal.

---

## Art. D.277 — Estacionamientos

One parking space per dwelling unit, commercial premises, office, or similar use.

---

## Art. D.277.1 — Volúmenes auxiliares

- Water tanks, ducts, roof/terrace treatments must be detailed in plans; Intendencia may condition or deny.
- **Absolute max height: 9.00 m** across entire sector (includes machinery rooms, roof access, pergolas, etc.).

---

## Art. D.276 — Salientes (projections/overhangs)

- Up to **1.50 m** projection permitted over main and rear facades at upper floor level.
- Min clearance: **2.40 m** above natural ground.
- Open terraces (3 sides open): do not count toward FOT.
- Open terraces (2 sides open): count 50% toward FOT.

---

## Art. D.277.3 — Zona 2.1 Costera Sur

Framework zone containing subzones 2.1.1 (Pueblo), 2.1.2 (Barrios Jardín), 2.1.3 (Resto). Rules come from the subzone articles.

---

## Art. D.279 — Subzona 2.1.1.1 Amanzanado Fundacional

**Scope:** Damero (checkerboard grid) on the José Ignacio peninsula + manzanas 74, 75, 76, 77. Excludes manzanas 34, 28, 22 (fragility zone).

### Height
- Max height: **6.00 m** from natural ground
- Ridge (sloped roof): **6.90 m** max, provided roof average ≤ 6.00 m
- Absolute cap (auxiliary): **9.00 m** (Art. D.277.1)

### Setbacks
- Front: **4.00 m**
- Front (coast/public spaces, mzna. 77): **6.00 m**
- Front (ocean frontage): **20.00 m**
- Bilateral (laterals): **2.00 m** each side
- Between volumes: **2.50 m** minimum separation

### Occupation factors
| Factor | Isolated | Paired |
|--------|----------|--------|
| FOS | 35% | 30% |
| FOT | 60% | 60% |
| Natural unpaved | ≥ 50% | ≥ 50% |

### Minimum lot requirements
| Type | Min. area | Min. frontage |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Isolated unit | 400 m² | 9 m |
| Paired units | 800 m² | 14 m per pair |

### Max linear development
- Ground floor: **20.00 m**
- Upper floor: **16.00 m**

### Projections
- Max 1.50 m (see Art. D.276)

## Art. D.280 — Subzona 2.1.1.2 Manzanas de fragilidad ecosistémica

**Scope:** Manzanas 34, 28, 22 only.

### Special requirements
- Mandatory prior consultation with topographic plans showing coastal dune crests
- Construction on **pilotes** (pilings): spaced > 2.50 m, min 1 m height, max 30 cm diameter
- No lawn, no dune coverage; only native coastal plants
- No fencing
- No impermeabilization or paving
- Wastewater only via public sewage network

### Height
- Max: **6.00 m** measured from natural ground + 1 m (i.e., measured from 1 m above ground due to pilotes)

### Setbacks
- Front: **4.00 m** (6 m along coast/public spaces)
- Lateral: **2.00 m** each
- Between volumes: **2.50 m**

### Occupation factors
| Factor | Isolated | Paired |
|--------|----------|--------|
| FOS | 35% | 30% |
| FOT | 60% | 60% |
| Natural unpaved | ≥ 60% | ≥ 60% |

### Max linear development
- Ground: 20.00 m
- Upper: 16.00 m

## Art. D.282 — Subzona 2.1.2.1 Áreas balnearias urbanizadas

**Scope:** All urban land areas in the sector, including named subdivisions (Faro Bahía, Santa María de los Médanos, Arenas de José Ignacio, others).

### Height
- Max: **7.00 m** above Recta de Comparación
- Ridge (sloped roof): **8.05 m** if average ≤ 7.00 m
- Floors: 2 (PB + PA)

### Setbacks
- Front (coast/public spaces): **6.00 m**
- Front (other): **4.00 m**
- Lateral: **2.00 m** each
- Rear: **3.00 m**
- Between volumes: **3.00 m**
- Secondary structures in lateral setbacks: inner 1/3 of depth only, max 20 m², max 3 m high

### Occupation factors
| Factor | Isolated | Paired |
|--------|----------|--------|
| FOS | 25% | 20% |
| FOT | 50% | 40% |
| Natural unpaved | ≥ 50% | ≥ 50% |

### Minimum lot requirements
| Type | Min. area | Min. frontage |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Isolated unit | 600 m² | — |
| Paired units | 1,200 m² | 20 m |
| Paired unit groups | 1,200 m² | 25 m/pair |

### Max linear development
- Ground floor: **20.00 m**
- Upper floor: **16.00 m**

### Fences

- Other boundaries: **1.30 m**

## Art. D.283 — Subzona 2.1.2.2 La Juanita

**Scope:** El fraccionamiento de la localidad Playa Juanita y la totalidad de la manzana catastral No. 19.

### Special block restrictions
In manzanas 3, 4, 26, 27, 40, 41, 48-52, 54-62, 68-71: **single-family homes prohibited on lots > 1,000 m²** (must use denser typologies: low-rise blocks, paired units).

### Height
- Max: **7.00 m** above Recta de Comparación
- Ridge (sloped roof): **8.05 m** if average ≤ 7.00 m
- Low-rise blocks: **9.00 m**
- Floors: 2 (PB + PA)

### Setbacks
- Front: **4.00 m** (10 m facing national routes)
- Lateral: **2.00 m** each
- Rear: **3.00 m**
- Between volumes: **4.00 m** (isolated) / **12.00 m** (paired)
- Secondary structures in lateral setbacks: inner 1/3 of depth, max 20 m², max 3 m high

### Occupation factors
| Factor | Isolated | Paired / Blocks |
|--------|----------|-----------------|
| FOS | 35% | 30% |
| FOT | 70% | 60% |
| Natural unpaved | ≥ 50% | ≥ 50% |

### Minimum lot requirements
| Type | Min. area | Min. frontage |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Isolated unit | 400 m² | 12 m |
| Paired units | 800 m² | 24 m |
| Low-rise blocks | 1,000 m² | 30 m |

### Max linear development
- Ground floor: **20.00 m**
- Upper floor: **16.00 m**

### Projections
- Max 1.50 m (see Art. D.276)

### Fences
- Front: **0.80 m**
- Other boundaries: **1.30 m**
