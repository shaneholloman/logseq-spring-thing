# San Carlos — Zona 1.4 Barrio Jardín (Urbano/Suburbano)

## Source Regulations
- **Art. 236, Decreto 3718/1997 (TONE)** — original Zone 1.4
- **Decreto 3876/2010** — first modification (added subzones)
- **Decreto 3982/2017** — refined subzones, added residential complex provision
- **Decreto 4042/2021** — latest modification (added Costanera Sur subzone)
- **Decreto 3904/2012** — lot-size-dependent FOS interpolation

## Zone Boundaries
Fraccionamiento San Carlos, extending to both sides of Ruta 104, up to calle Las Pindó (west of Ruta 104) and Avenida Las Ballenas (east of Ruta 104), excluding properties in Zones 1.2 and 1.3.

## Subzones (Decreto 4042/2021)

### Subzona 1.4.1 — Costanera Norte
- **Boundary:** Urban/suburban parcels north of Ruta 10, fronting the route, between eastern boundary of San Carlos subdivision and Ruta 104/Av. Las Ballenas
- **Permitted types:** Low Blocks (Bloques Bajos) only
- **Min lot:** 2,000 m² area, 30 m frontage (for blocks); 1,200 m² (individual)
- **Max height:** 9–12 m (PB + 2 pisos)
- **Setbacks:** 10 m front (Ruta 10), 6 m (cross streets), 3 m lateral (sum > 8m), 4 m rear
- **FOS:** 25–40% (depending on lot size)
- **FOT:** 70%
- Blocks must face Ruta 10 with 100% facade development
- Max 900 m² per floor, inscribed in 42.50 m diameter circle

### Subzona 1.4.2 — Resto
- **Boundary:** All remaining properties in Zone 1.4
- **Permitted types:** Isolated individual or paired residential units only (no blocks)
- **Min lot:** 1,000 m² per dwelling, 6 m separation between units
- **Max height:** 7 m (PB + 1 piso)
- **Setbacks:** 6 m front (4 m originally; 10 m if on Ruta 10), 3 m + 2 m lateral, 3 m rear
- **FOS:** 40% (lots < 400 m²) to 25% (lots > 1,000 m²), interpolate between
- **FOSSS:** 25–40% (same interpolation)
- **FOSV:** 50%
- **FOT:** 60% (lots < 400 m²) to 50% (lots > 1,000 m²)
- For lots with frontage ≤ 15m: unilateral 3 m setback, 2 m lateral waived (max 3 m height in waived area)

### Subzona 1.4.3 — Costanera Sur
- **Boundary:** Urban/suburban parcels south of Ruta 10, fronting the route, between padrón 18156 and padrón 26633
- **Permitted types:** Low Buildings (2+ units)
- **Min lot:** 2,000 m² area, 30 m frontage
- **Max height:** 7 m
- **Setbacks:** 10 m front (Ruta 10), 6 m lateral (sum > 12 m)
- **FOS:** 25%
- **FOT:** 50%

## Auxiliary Constructions in Setbacks (all subzones)
- Max 36 m² in lateral and rear setbacks
- Max 3 m average height
- Rear half of lot only (or middle third for triple-frontage)
- Includes: storage, garages, covered grills, service rooms

## Overhangs (Salientes)
- Up to 2 m projection on max 30% of front/rear facade surface

## Ground-Level Garage Bonus
- Additional 10% FOS permitted at ground level for garage
- Not counted toward FOT

## Optional Residential Complex (Decreto 3982/2017)
For suburban land ≥ 20 hectares:
- Blocks within 42.50 m diameter circles
- 8,000 m² per block
- 20 m inter-block spacing
- 20 m front / 15 m side and rear setbacks
- 12 m max height
- 40% FOT

## General Provisions
- Rooftop services (barbecue, bathroom, kitchenette) limited to 20% of roof area, max 2.60 m interior height (Decreto 3876)
- Basement garages/services not counted toward FOT (Decreto 3876)
- Water tanks may exceed max height with tolerance request (Art. 230)
- Property boundary demarcation required per fence regulations (Art. 232)
